Delete Your Data

Clipmap · developer: ishankgp

Clipmap is the app published on Google Play under the package com.ishankgp.videomap. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open Clipmap.
  2. Tap Profile (bottom navigation bar).
  3. Tap Delete my data, then tap again to confirm.

This immediately and permanently deletes your account and all of your data. It cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you can't access the app, email us from any address with the subject "Delete my Clipmap data". We complete verified requests within 30 days.

ankitpangy@gmail.com

What is deleted

  • Your anonymous account identifier (your Clipmap guest account).
  • All of your saved places and collections.
  • Your reel-processing (curation) job records.
  • Your subscription entitlement record held by us.
  • Product-analytics events associated with you.

What is kept

  • Shared place reference data (e.g. a restaurant's name and map location) that is not personal to you and may be referenced by other users.
  • Aggregated/anonymized analytics that can no longer identify you.
  • Billing records that Google Play and RevenueCat are required to retain for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Retention period

In-app deletion takes effect immediately. Email requests are completed within 30 days. Backups containing residual copies are cycled out within 90 days.
